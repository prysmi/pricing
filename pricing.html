Financial Services Case Study

FinTech Leader Doubles Efficiency

A detailed look at how we transformed a global FinTech's manual processes into a streamlined, automated revenue engine.

2x Operational Efficiency
$1.5M Annual Savings
6 Months To Full Implementation
100% User Adoption

The Customer Journey

error

The Challenge: Revenue Bottlenecks

The client, a rapidly growing FinTech, was crippled by complex manual processes for underwriting and compliance. This led to significant delays, a high error rate, and revenue bottlenecks that threatened their growth trajectory. Their existing Salesforce instance was under-utilized and not configured to handle their unique workflow.

checklist

The Solution: Strategic Automation

Our journey began with a deep-dive audit. We didn't just look at code; we interviewed stakeholders from sales, legal, and operations to map the *real* business process. The solution was a multi-phased implementation:

  • Phase 1: Sales Cloud Optimization. We re-architected their opportunity workflow and automated lead routing.
  • Phase 2: Service Cloud for Compliance. We built a custom compliance-tracking system within Service Cloud, using automation to flag issues in real-time.
  • Phase 3: Process Automation (Flow). We replaced 80% of their manual data entry with Salesforce Flow, creating a "zero-touch" process for standard approvals.
bar_chart

The Result: Efficiency Unlocked

Within six months, the transformation was complete. The underwriting process time was reduced from 3 days to 4 hours. The $1.5M in annual savings came directly from re-allocating 10,000+ manual hours per year. Most importantly, the sales team could now focus on closing deals, not fighting the system.
